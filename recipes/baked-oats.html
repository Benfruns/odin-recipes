Recipe: Baked Oats

These blueberry baked oats are a great breakfast.

Serves 8.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 & 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup chopped cashews
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 cups frozen blueberries

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees C
  2. Whisk eggs, add milk, honey, vanilla and olive oil. Mix until thoroughly combined
  3. Add cinnamon, oats, nuts, salt and baking poweder, stirring to combine
  4. Cover the base of a large baking dishwith blueberries, ensuring they are evenly distributed
  5. Pour the mixture over the blueberries and spread evenly
  6. Bake for 40 minutes or until golden brown

Serve with 1 tbsp of Greek Yoghurt.

Approximately 367 calories (30g carbs, 22g fat, 13g protein) per serve.
