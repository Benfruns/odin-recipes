Recipe: Muesli Bars

Great for a snack

Serves 12.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup pepitas
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/4 cup chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup shredded coconut
  • 1/4 dried apricots, chopped
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup tahini

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees C
  2. Melt coconut oil and tahini if hard
  3. Mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl, combine well
  4. Add mix to a lined baking tray, compressing the mix down firmly to help it hold together
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until brown
  6. Cool in fridge overnight before cutting into slices with a bread knife
